Free Online Image Compression Tool

Our image compression tool helps you reduce the file size of your images without sacrificing visual quality. Perfect for optimizing website images, reducing storage space, and improving page load times.

Why Compress Your Images?

  • Faster Website Loading: Compressed images load faster, improving user experience and SEO rankings.
  • Save Storage Space: Reduce image file sizes by up to 90% without noticeable quality loss.
  • Bandwidth Optimization: Lower data usage for mobile users and reduce hosting bandwidth costs.
  • Improved SEO: Search engines favor websites with faster loading times.
  • Social Media Ready: Optimize images for Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and other platforms.

How to Use Our Image Compressor

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking the browse button
  2. Adjust compression level using the quality slider (higher = better quality, lower = smaller file)
  3. Choose output format (JPG, PNG, WebP, or keep original)
  4. Click "Compress Image" to process your image
  5. Download the compressed image and compare file sizes

Supported Image Formats

Our tool supports all popular image formats including JPG/JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, TIFF, and WebP. WebP format provides superior compression compared to traditional formats while maintaining high quality.

Privacy Guaranteed

All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security for your sensitive files.
